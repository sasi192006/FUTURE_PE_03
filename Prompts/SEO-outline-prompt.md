# 🤖 SEO Blog Outline Generator Prompt

## 📌 Prompt Purpose
This prompt generates a **structured SEO blog outline (H1–H3)** for a coaching institute website.  
The goal is to create content that **matches search intent, improves Google rankings, and attracts students looking for coaching institutes.**

The outline will later be used to generate **full blog articles** for the content cluster strategy.

---

# 🚀 Prompt

You are an **expert SEO strategist and professional education content writer**.

Your task is to generate a **detailed SEO-optimized blog outline** for a coaching institute website that helps students preparing for competitive exams.

---

## 🏢 Business Information

Business Type: Coaching Institute  
Target Audience: Students and parents  
Location: [Insert City]

Examples:

- Hyderabad  
- Bangalore  
- Delhi  

---

## 🎯 Blog Topic

[Insert Blog Topic]

Example topics:

- Best Coaching Institute in [City]
- IIT JEE Coaching in [City]
- NEET Coaching in [City]
- Benefits of Coaching for Competitive Exams
- How to Choose the Best Coaching Institute

---

## 🔑 Target Keyword

Example:

Best Coaching Institute in Hyderabad

---

## 📊 Requirements

Generate a **complete SEO blog outline** that includes:

1. **One clear H1 heading**
2. **Multiple H2 sections explaining the topic**
3. **Relevant H3 subtopics for deeper explanation**
4. **Sections that match student search intent**
5. **FAQ section with common student questions**
6. **Suggestions for internal links to related blogs**
7. **A conclusion that encourages students to enroll**

The outline should be designed for a **1000–1500 word blog article**.

---

## 🧠 SEO Considerations

The outline should:

- target **local SEO keywords**
- answer **common student questions**
- provide **clear and helpful information**
- build **trust with parents and students**
- encourage **enrollment in the coaching institute**

---

## 📚 Expected Outline Structure

H1: Blog Title

H2: Introduction to the Topic

H2: Why Coaching Institutes Are Important for Competitive Exams

H2: Courses Offered by Coaching Institutes  
H3: IIT JEE Coaching  
H3: NEET Coaching  
H3: Foundation Courses for School Students  

H2: Key Features of a Good Coaching Institute  
H3: Experienced Faculty  
H3: Structured Study Material  
H3: Regular Mock Tests  

H2: Benefits of Joining a Coaching Institute

H2: Tips for Students Preparing for Competitive Exams

H2: Frequently Asked Questions

H2: Conclusion

---

## 🎨 Writing Style

The outline should be:

- clear and structured
- SEO optimized
- student-friendly
- suitable for a coaching institute website

---

## 📄 Output Format

Return the result in **clean Markdown format** with headings clearly labeled as:

H1, H2, and H3.

The outline should be **ready to convert into a full blog article.**
