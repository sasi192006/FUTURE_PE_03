# 🤖 Supporting Blog Generation Prompt (Coaching Institute)

## Prompt Purpose
This prompt generates **supporting SEO blog articles** that strengthen the main **pillar blog** and help the coaching institute rank for multiple related keywords.

Supporting blogs target **specific student questions and search queries** and link back to the main pillar blog.

---

# 🚀 Prompt

You are an **SEO expert and educational content writer** creating content for a **coaching institute website**.

Your task is to write a **detailed SEO supporting blog article** that helps students and parents understand coaching options for competitive exams.

---

## 📌 Blog Topic

[Insert Blog Topic]

Examples:

- IIT JEE Coaching in [City]
- NEET Coaching in [City]
- How to Choose the Best Coaching Institute
- Benefits of Coaching for Competitive Exams
- Tips to Prepare for Competitive Exams

---

## 🎯 Target Audience

- Students preparing for competitive exams
- Parents looking for coaching institutes
- high school and college students

---

## 🔑 Target Keyword

Example:

IIT JEE Coaching in Hyderabad

---

## ✍️ Requirements

Write a **1000–1200 word SEO optimized blog article** that:

1. Uses **clear headings (H1, H2, H3)**.
2. Naturally includes **location-based keywords for local SEO**.
3. Explains the topic in a **clear and easy-to-understand way for students**.
4. Provides **useful tips, insights, and guidance**.
5. Builds **trust and credibility for the coaching institute**.
6. Includes **examples or explanations where useful**.
7. Adds a **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) section**.
8. Suggests **internal linking to the pillar blog**.
9. Ends with a **strong call-to-action encouraging students to enroll or contact the institute**.

---

## 🧠 Blog Structure

H1: [Blog Title]

H2: Introduction to the Topic

H2: Why This Coaching is Important

H2: Key Features of Good Coaching Institutes
H3: Experienced Faculty
H3: Structured Study Material
H3: Regular Mock Tests

H2: Tips for Students Preparing for the Exam

H2: How to Choose the Right Coaching Institute

H2: Frequently Asked Questions

H2: Conclusion

---

## 🎨 Writing Style

- Student-friendly
- clear and informative
- engaging and helpful
- professional but simple
- SEO optimized but natural

---

## 📎 Additional Instructions

At the end of the blog include:

- Suggested **internal links to the pillar blog**  
- Suggested **links to other supporting blogs**  
- A **call-to-action encouraging students to join the coaching institute**

---

## 📄 Output Format

Return the blog in **clean Markdown format** so it can be directly published on a website or stored in a GitHub repository.
