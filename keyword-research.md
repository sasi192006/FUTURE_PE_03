# 🧠 Search Intent Analysis

Search intent refers to **the reason why a user searches for a keyword**.

Understanding this helps create content that **matches user needs and ranks better on Google.**

### Primary Keyword Intent

Keyword: **Best Digital Marketing Agency in Hyderabad**

User intent:

- Compare different agencies
- Understand services offered
- Check pricing
- Find a trustworthy marketing partner

The pillar blog should therefore:

- explain digital marketing services
- provide pricing insights
- highlight benefits of hiring an agency
- help users choose the right agency

---

# 📊 Keyword Strategy

This project uses a **Content Cluster Strategy**.

Structure:

Primary Keyword → Pillar Blog  
Supporting Keywords → Supporting Blogs

Example:

Best Digital Marketing Agency in Hyderabad
│
├── SEO Services in Hyderabad
├── Social Media Marketing in Hyderabad
├── Digital Marketing Cost in Hyderabad
└── How to Choose a Digital Marketing Agency

This structure improves:

- SEO rankings
- topical authority
- internal linking
- website traffic
